About the Festival

Discover the mission, vision, and strategic objectives behind Tanzania Traditional Dances Festival 2025

Introduction

The Centre for Sports Research and Governance (CSRG), in its patriotic commitment to foster national harmony, inclusive development, and cultural diplomacy, proudly presents the Tanzania Traditional Dances Festival (TTDF)—a historic and unifying cultural event.

Scheduled for 4th-5th September 2025 in Dodoma, the TTDF brings together traditional dances troupes from all 31 administrative regions of Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar, symbolizing the richness, diversity, and unity of our great nation.

This festival is a celebration of Umoja wa Kitaifa—a tribute to the foundations of our Union between Tanzania Mainland and Zanzibar.

Unity in Diversity

Bringing together all 31 regions of Tanzania and Zanzibar in a celebration of our shared cultural heritage and national identity.

General Objective

The Centre for Sports Research and Governance Limited (CSRG) recognizes this cultural asset and has decided to organize the "Tanzania Traditional Dances Festival 2025: Celebrating Unity and Peace through Cultural Heritage Ahead of the General Election." The festival will act as a strategic platform to celebrate our Cultural Heritage, invigorate the spirit of togetherness, and promote peace and tranquility.

Specific Objectives

Promote Unity

Promote unity, patriotism, and cultural identity through traditional dances

Strengthen Peace

Use traditional dances as a tool of strengthening peace and harmony ahead of the 2025 Tanzania General Election

Showcase Diversity

Provide a national platform for showcasing diverse tribal dances

Empower Youth

Empower youth and traditional dance groups
