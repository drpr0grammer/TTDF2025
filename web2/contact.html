Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Tanzania Traditional Dances Festival 2025.

How can I participate in the festival?

Participation is open to traditional dance troupes from all 31 regions of Tanzania and Zanzibar. Contact CSRG for registration details and requirements.

Is the festival open to the public?

Yes, the festival is open to the public. We expect 1,500 to 2,000 attendees. Entry details will be announced closer to the event date.

What are the accommodation options?

Accommodation will be provided for participating groups. For visitors, we recommend booking hotels in Dodoma well in advance.

How can I sponsor the festival?

We welcome sponsorships from organizations and individuals. Contact us for sponsorship packages and opportunities.

Will there be food and refreshments?

Yes, food and refreshments will be available at the venue. We'll also showcase traditional Tanzanian cuisine.

Is photography allowed?

Photography is encouraged! However, please be respectful of performers and follow any specific guidelines provided at the venue.
